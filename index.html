The Problem

Most bookmark tools are frustrating to use.

Cluttered interfaces, slow loading, privacy concerns, and features you don't need. Your bookmarks deserve better than being buried in a mess of unused tools and ads.

The Solution

A clean, fast workspace for your most important sites.

Every new tab becomes a customizable grid of your favorite sites. Beautiful icons, perfect layouts, instant access. Built by developers, for developers.

Layouts that fit your workflow

Choose from presets or customize columns, gaps, and tile sizes to match exactly how you work — not how someone else thinks you should.

Themes that feel like home

Light, dark, or system-following themes with ten accent colors and a custom color picker. Make it yours.

Icons that make sense

Auto-fetched favicons plus a curated collection of beautiful icons. Search by name and find the perfect visual instantly.

Your data stays yours

Everything runs locally in your browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Backup and restore your setup as simple JSON.

Keyboard-first, mouse-optional

Search, open tabs, sort, and tag with keyboard shortcuts. Get things done without reaching for the mouse.

Multiple workspaces, zero friction

Create separate tab groups for different contexts — coding, research, side projects. Switch between them instantly.
