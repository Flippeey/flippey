How it works

Your browser bookmarks, unleashed.

Seven things that change how you use bookmarks — led by Workspaces. Hover to explore, or watch them cycle.

  • One browser, many Workspaces Turn any folder into its own themed space — work, AI, design, gaming. Switch in one click; nothing bleeds between them.
  • Set up in under a minute The wizard reads your existing bookmarks and suggests ready-made Workspaces — accept and you're done
  • See your bookmarks your way Flip between a visual grid and a dense, scannable list in one click — same bookmarks, two minds
  • A look for every Workspace Light or dark, ten accent colors, and rich gradient backgrounds — set per Workspace, not globally
  • Find any bookmark in two keystrokes Fuzzy search across every Workspace and folder, with the folder path on each hit so you always know where it lives
  • Reorder by instinct Grab anything and drop it where it belongs — multi-select to move whole groups into folders at once
  • Make every tile recognisable Don't like an auto icon? Search the built-in icon library or paste your own — per bookmark, in seconds
